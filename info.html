PROGRESSOR - Guide

Achieve your best in academics with these key practices

Enthusiasm

  • Extra HW: Go beyond assigned homework by doing extra practice.
  • Open for Asking: Be open to asking any doubts to deepen your understanding.
  • Chapter Requests: Request your teachers to cover topics you find intriguing.
  • Recap: Don't hesitate to ask for a recap if you forget something.
  • Responsive Interaction: Engage in responsive interactions during class.

Brilliance

  • Critical Thinking: Enhance analytical skills with critical thinking questions.
  • Thorough Homework: Complete all assignments thoroughly for better understanding.
  • Good Test Scores: Aim for good marks in tests and examinations.
  • Active Participation: Pay attention in class and answer questions correctly.
  • Remember Formulas: Retain important formulas and key points from lectures.

Capacity

  • Multiple Subjects: Explore more than one favorite subject to broaden interests.
  • Balanced Results: Aim for balanced performance in all subject chapters.
  • Versatility: Strive for similar marks in all subjects to demonstrate versatility.

Obedience

  • Task Completion: Complete all homework and assigned tasks diligently.
  • Punctuality: Submit homework on time to demonstrate punctuality.
  • Thorough Preparation: Be fully prepared for tests and examinations.
  • Follow Instructions: Listen carefully and follow instructions attentively.

Discipline

  • Regular HW: Establish a routine for completing homework regularly.
  • Planned Study Time: Plan study time in advance and stick to the schedule.
  • Punctual in Class: Be on time for classes and ready to learn.
  • Stay Focused: Avoid distractions and maintain focus during class.
